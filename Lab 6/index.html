Welcome to Vera Bradleys Sunny New Styles and The New Spring Collection

Vera Bradley has certainly earned a reputation as a leader in the gift industry. She has done so because of her stylish quilted cotton luggage, handbags, eyewear, stationaryand even accessories. This company combines smart product designs with distinctive and colorful fabrics and trims. The company is located in Fort Wayne, Indiana. But they also have a story.

So the story goes while on vacation in March of 1982, Patricia and Barbara were awaiting a flight in Atlanta when they noticed a definite lack of feminine looking luggage. The longtime friends wasted no time in correcting this situation. Within weeks these dynamic woman had created a company named after Barbaras mother, capable of marketing and manufacturing their cleverly designed products.Since then the demand for these unique handbag and luggage items and more has grown and so has Vera Bradley. This company is now nationaly and internationally recognized.
